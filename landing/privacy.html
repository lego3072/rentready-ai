2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Generate condition reports from your uploaded photographs
  • Deliver reports: Send reports via email, generate download links, enable sharing
  • Account management: Authenticate you, manage subscriptions, track report history
  • Transactional emails: Send email verification, password reset links, and report deliveries
  • Improve the Service: Understand usage patterns to improve performance and features
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

3. AI Processing

When you upload photographs, they are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered analysis. This is the core of how our Service works.

  • Photographs are transmitted securely (HTTPS) to Anthropic's servers for processing
  • Anthropic processes the images to generate condition assessments and returns the results to us
  • We do not use your photographs to train AI models
  • Anthropic's data handling is governed by their privacy policy and API terms of service

4. Data Retention

  • Uploaded photographs: Automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours
  • Generated PDF reports: Automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours
  • Share links: Active for 7 days after creation
  • Account data (email, name, report metadata): Retained until you request deletion
  • Email/password reset tokens: Expire after 24 hours (verification) or 1 hour (reset) and are deleted

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use traditional cookies. Instead, the Service uses:

  • localStorage: Stores your device fingerprint (for session management) and account data (for login persistence). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to third parties.
  • sessionStorage: Temporarily stores UTM marketing parameters. Cleared when you close the browser tab.

Third-party services (Google Ads, Cloudflare, Stripe) may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Passwords stored using bcrypt hashing (never plaintext)
  • Rate limiting on all sensitive endpoints
  • Security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options)
  • Automatic file deletion after 24 hours
  • Database connection pooling with secure credentials

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
